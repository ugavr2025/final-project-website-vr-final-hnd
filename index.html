About ClassVR

ClassVR is an immersive virtual reality learning platform that transports students into a shared digital classroom, replacing isolating video calls with a true sense of presence.

Video Here

Motivation

One of the courses that would benefit from collaboration and guidance but was difficult to administer such activities over virtual environments is mathematics. With professors unable to actively see students' progress and catch mistakes quicker, and without a streamlined process for students to collaborate with other on math problems, similar to how some lectures are conducted in traditional classroom environments, many students found it difficult to actively engage with the material or get unstuck on math problems in isolated environments.

ClassVR focuses on provides students a virtual, collaborative platform to engage with other students and the professor in an environment that mimics the environment of a traditional classroom. With a class whiteboard, professors can see where students are at when solving problems, and students can feel as if they are learning in a classroom. ClassVR breaks the isolation of virtual classes and bridges minds together to make virtual learning engaging.

Features

Classroom Environment

Upon joining the classroom, students can find their seat, similar to how students enter a traditional classroom. Each student is given an avatar in the virtual environment.

Students can view the professor's screen or camera, where professors can give lectures. The classroom is also built to have active learning, where professors and students can flip through a set of questions on the board, and students have access to a shared whiteboard to solve math problems.

Monitoring Progress

Professors can use the headset and join the classroom to monitor students' progress and engagement.

AI Assistant

To help students get assistance as efficient and effective as possible, an AI bot is available for students to use. Students can go to the bot and verbally ask questions, and the bot will respond back with helpful suggestions and clarifications on concepts.

Accessibility

The environment is set up to be accessible in a sitting manner, rather than needing to stand and move around within the home environment.
